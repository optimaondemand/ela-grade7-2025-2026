# Module 1 Outro Quiz — Canvas Survey Setup

## Quiz Settings
- **Title:** Module 1 Outro — The Odyssey
- **Type:** Graded Survey (5 pts completion — signals module completion for gradebook)
- **Points:** 5
- **Allowed attempts:** 1
- **Show correct answers:** No (survey)
- **Available from:** End of Week 5 (set availability date accordingly)

---

## Quiz Questions

**Q1 — Essential Question: Final Response** (Essay question)

*At the start of this module you were asked: "What makes someone a hero? Is it what they do, who they are, or how their story is told?"*

*Now that you have read The Odyssey and written your module project, answer the same question — but draw specifically on the poem. Write 3–5 sentences. Your answer should be more specific and more argued than your Week 1 response.*

---

**Q2 — Closing Confidence** (Likert scale)

*How confident are you now in your ability to write an analytical essay that takes and defends a position?*

1 — Not confident at all
2 — Slightly confident
3 — Somewhat confident
4 — Fairly confident
5 — Very confident

---

**Q3 — What Remains Unclear?** (Short answer — teacher early-warning signal)

*Is there anything from this module you feel you didn't fully understand — a concept, a scene from the poem, a grammar technique, or something about how to write analysis? Write 1–3 sentences. Be honest. Your teacher reads these responses to plan what to review.*

---

**Q4 — Virtue Reflection** (Essay question)

*The module's seven virtues include courage, honesty, perseverance, and self-government. Which of these did this module's work require most from you — and how?*

Write 2–3 sentences naming a specific moment in your work (a VR session, a quiz, the project draft) where that virtue was tested.

---

**Q5 — One Thing That Surprised You** (Short answer)

*What is one thing you learned or noticed in The Odyssey that genuinely surprised you? It can be about the poem, about analysis, about your own writing, or about the VR work.*

---

## Teacher Analytics Note

This outro generates four data streams for the teacher to triage in SpeedGrader:

1. **Q1 (Essential Question):** Compare to Q1 responses from the intro quiz. Students whose responses are as vague as Week 1 may need individual follow-up.
2. **Q2 (Confidence rating):** Sort by rating. Students at 1–2 at module end are flagged for check-in. Run this triage in ~15 min across the cohort.
3. **Q3 (What remains unclear):** Scan for recurring themes. If more than 15% of students name the same concept, address it in a brief module-wide announcement before Module 2 begins.
4. **Q4 (Virtue):** Not scored — used for formation data and community-building. Optional: share anonymized responses in the opening of Module 2 as a community-building moment.
