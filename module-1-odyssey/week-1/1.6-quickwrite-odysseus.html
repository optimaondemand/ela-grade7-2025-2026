The Odyssey — Week 1 — Quick Write

Quick Write 1.6 — What Kind of Man Is Odysseus?

Building your first analytical paragraph about character

What This Builds

This Quick Write is the seed of your module project essay. By the end of this module you will write 3–4 paragraphs arguing whether Odysseus’s cunning is ultimately virtuous or destructive. That argument depends on a close understanding of who Odysseus is — which is what you are building right now. Save this paragraph; you will return to it in Week 5 when you draft your full essay.

Prompt
Based on Books 1 and 5 of The Odyssey: What kind of man is Odysseus? Write one paragraph (5–7 sentences) that describes his character. Every claim you make must be supported by a specific detail from the text — not your general impression, but something Homer actually shows you. Begin with a sentence that makes a clear claim about who Odysseus is. End with a sentence that raises a question you still have about him.
Instructions

Completion Criteria

This assignment is graded on completion (5 points). You receive full credit if:

  • Your paragraph is at least 5 sentences long
  • You make at least one specific reference to something in Books 1 or 5
