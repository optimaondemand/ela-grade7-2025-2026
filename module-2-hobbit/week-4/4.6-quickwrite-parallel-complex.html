The Hobbit — Week 4 — Quick Write

Quick Write 4.6 — Parallel Structure and Complex Sentences

Writing about moral choice using this week’s grammar structures

What You’re Doing

This quick write asks you to write a paragraph of 5–7 sentences about Bilbo’s decision to give away the Arkenstone. Your paragraph must include: one sentence with correct parallel structure (series or paired conjunction), one complex sentence with a concession (although/even though), and one complex sentence expressing cause or condition. All three sentences must be labeled.

Your Paragraph

Topic: Was Bilbo right to give away the Arkenstone? Argue your position.

Your paragraph should state a clear position, support it with at least two specific details from the novel, and acknowledge what the other side would say (this is where your concession sentence will go). Label your three required grammar structures in brackets at the end of each sentence.
