7th Grade ELA — Module 4
Roll of Thunder, Hear My Cry — Week 3 — Quickwrite 3.6

QW 3.6: Two Characters, One Argument

5–7 sentences using the Hammer-Papa foil to argue what Taylor believes about resistance

The Prompt

In 5–7 sentences, use Papa and Uncle Hammer as a foil pair to make an argument about what Taylor believes about resistance to injustice. You are not summarizing what each character does — you are arguing what the comparison reveals. What does Taylor seem to think about patience? About anger? About which approach is right?

Required Tags
  • [CC] — One compound-complex sentence. Label it in the text.
  • [FOIL] — The word FOIL used analytically — not just as a label, but in a sentence that explains what the foil does or reveals.
  • [ARG] — A sentence that states the specific argument you think Taylor is making through this pairing. Not “Taylor shows different responses to injustice” — that’s a topic. Your [ARG] should be a claim about what Taylor believes, argues, or wants readers to understand.
Submission

Type your response directly into the Canvas text box. Label your tags in brackets. 5 points, graded on completion and required tags present.
