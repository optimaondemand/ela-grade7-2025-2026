7th Grade ELA — Module 4
Roll of Thunder, Hear My Cry — Week 4 — Quickwrite 4.6

QW 4.6: The Novel’s Argument

5–7 sentences stating and supporting the novel’s central theme

The Prompt

In 5–7 sentences, state what you believe is the central THEME of Roll of Thunder, Hear My Cry — not the topic (racial injustice, family, land) but the specific claim Taylor makes about it. What does she argue about how people hold on to what is theirs? Use at least one scene from the novel as evidence.

Required Tags
  • [RQ] — One rhetorical question that opens your argument or drives it forward. Label it.
  • [CON] — One concessive sentence that acknowledges a complication or objection to your theme claim before answering it. Label it.
  • [THM] — The word THEME used in a sentence that states the novel’s specific claim — not a topic, but an argument. “The theme is racial injustice” is a topic, not a theme. “The theme is that holding on to dignity sometimes requires knowing when to spend what you have been protecting” is a claim.
Submission

Type your response directly into the Canvas text box. Label your tags in brackets. 5 points, graded on completion and required tags present.
