7th Grade ELA — Module 5
The Diary of a Young Girl — Week 1 — Quickwrite 1.6

QW 1.6: Anne’s Voice

5–7 sentences arguing what Anne’s voice reveals about her character

The Prompt

In 5–7 sentences, argue what Anne’s VOICE in the opening weeks of her diary reveals about her character. Don’t just describe how she sounds — argue what her way of writing tells you about who she is and what she values. Use at least one specific example from the diary (a phrase, a sentence, or a moment).

Required Tags
  • [DASH] — One sentence using an em dash. Label it. The dash should add something that matters — not just length.
  • [VOICE] — The word VOICE used analytically in a sentence that makes a specific claim. Not “her voice is interesting” but what her voice argues about who she is.
  • [EV] — One specific example from the diary text (a phrase, a scene, or a choice she makes). Label it.
Submission

Type your response directly into the Canvas text box. Label your tags in brackets. 5 points, graded on completion and required tags present.
