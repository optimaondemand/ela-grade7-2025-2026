7th Grade ELA — Module 5
The Diary of a Young Girl — Week 2 — Quickwrite 2.6

QW 2.6: What Confinement Cannot Erase

5–7 sentences arguing what the diary reveals about what cannot be taken

The Prompt

In 5–7 sentences, argue what the diary’s middle entries reveal about what the Annex and the Nazi occupation could not erase from Anne Frank. Connect this to the module’s essential question. Use at least one specific example from the diary entries you have read this week.

Required Tags
  • [PARA] — One sentence using parallel structure (a list of three or more items in the same grammatical form). Label it.
  • [DASH] — One sentence using an em dash for emphasis or pivot. Label it.
  • [EQ] — One sentence directly connecting your argument to the essential question: How do people preserve who they are when everything around them tries to erase them?
Submission

Type your response directly into the Canvas text box. Label your tags in brackets. 5 points, graded on completion and required tags present.
