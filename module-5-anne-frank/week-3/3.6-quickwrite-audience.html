7th Grade ELA — Module 5
The Diary of a Young Girl — Week 3 — Quickwrite 3.6

QW 3.6: Anne’s Audience

5–7 sentences arguing how Anne’s sense of audience changed her writing

The Prompt

In 5–7 sentences, argue how Anne’s decision to revise her diary for a future reader changed what the diary is and what it does. Use the word AUDIENCE analytically. Include at least one quotation from the diary integrated with a signal verb other than “says.”

Required Tags
  • [AUD] — The word AUDIENCE used analytically (not just mentioned). A sentence that makes a specific claim about what audience does to writing.
  • [Q] — One quotation from the diary, correctly integrated with a signal verb other than “says.” Label the signal verb [SIGNAL] as well.
  • [DASH] — One sentence using an em dash.
Submission

Type your response directly into the Canvas text box. Label your tags in brackets. 5 points, graded on completion and required tags present.
