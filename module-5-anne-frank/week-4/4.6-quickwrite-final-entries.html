7th Grade ELA — Module 5
The Diary of a Young Girl — Week 4 — Quickwrite 4.6

QW 4.6: What the Diary Proves

5–7 sentences arguing what the diary, as a whole, demonstrates about the module question

The Prompt

In 5–7 sentences, argue what The Diary of a Young Girl, taken as a whole, demonstrates about the question: How do people preserve who they are when everything around them tries to erase them? Include at least one quotation from the final entries of the diary, correctly integrated with a signal verb.

Required Tags
  • [PERIODIC] — One periodic sentence. It may be your opening or closing sentence.
  • [Q][SIGNAL] — One quotation from the diary integrated with a signal verb other than “says.” Label both.
  • [PARA] — One parallel list of three items (nouns, infinitives, or clauses).
Submission

Type your response directly into the Canvas text box. Label your tags in brackets. 5 points, graded on completion and required tags present.
