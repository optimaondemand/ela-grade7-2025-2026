**Question 1** (5 pts)
What is an allegory?

A. A story told entirely through dialogue between two or more characters.
B. A figure of speech comparing two unlike things using "like" or "as."
C. A narrative in which characters, settings, and events represent abstract ideas or real historical figures beyond the story's literal level.
