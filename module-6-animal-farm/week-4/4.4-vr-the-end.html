VR 4.4: Stage the Final Image

The pigs at the card table — and the animals watching from outside

What You’re Doing

Stage the final scene of Animal Farm: the pigs inside the farmhouse playing cards with the humans, and the other animals outside looking in through the window. Your composition should make an argument about what this image means — specifically, about what has and has not changed since the novel's opening. The irony you are trying to capture: the animals are in exactly the same position as at the beginning, looking at their oppressors from the outside.

What You’ll Need
  • Device: VR headset
  • Session ID: ELA7-M6-W4 — Persistent Session
  • Location: Domestic Interior or Manor House
  • Bring: Your reading of Chapter 10's final scene
What to Do
1

Build the final image (6–8 IFX)

Inside the Location: arrange IFX representing the pigs and humans at the card table — comfort, authority, wealth, human behavior. Outside the Location (or in a separate area representing the outside): arrange IFX representing the watching animals — the same position they were in at the novel's opening. The spatial arrangement should argue something about the circularity of the story.

2

Place a sticky note with an ironic contrast

Sticky note format: [WHEREAS] + one sentence naming what has changed since Chapter 1 and what has stayed exactly the same. Then [IRONY] + one sentence naming what type of irony this scene uses (verbal, dramatic, or structural) and what it argues.

3

Take a snapshot and submit

Name it YourLastName_M6_4.4. In the Canvas text box: one sentence using a contrast clause arguing what Orwell's final image reveals about the essential question.

Submission

Upload snapshot YourLastName_M6_4.4 + one-sentence Canvas text. Estimated time: 20–25 minutes in headset.

Facilitator Note

The strongest compositions will stage the inside/outside distinction so that the outside animals occupy the same relative position as at the novel's beginning — establishing the circular structure of the allegory. The Canvas text sentence is the key analytical artifact: check that it makes a claim about what the circularity argues about power, not just that it "shows things went back to how they started."
