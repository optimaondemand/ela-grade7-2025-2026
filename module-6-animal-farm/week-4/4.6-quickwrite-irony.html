Animal Farm — Quickwrite 4.6

Quickwrite 4.6: What the Final Image Argues

5 points — completion

The Prompt

Write 5–7 sentences arguing what Orwell's final image — the animals outside the farmhouse window, unable to tell pig from man — argues about the essential question: How does language shape who holds power, and who loses it?

This is the end of the novel. What does it prove about everything that came before it?

Required Tags
[WHEREAS] or [ALTHOUGH] — one contrast clause naming what has changed and what has stayed the same
[IRONY] — one sentence naming the type of irony in the final scene and arguing what it reveals
[EQ] — one sentence answering the essential question using the final scene as your evidence

Type directly into the Canvas text box. Your argument should be about what the final image proves — not just what it shows.
