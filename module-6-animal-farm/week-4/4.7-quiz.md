**Question 1** (5 pts)
What is anaphora?

A. The use of a word that imitates a sound (e.g., "buzz," "crack").
B. The repetition of the same word or phrase at the beginning of successive clauses or sentences, creating rhythm and emotional momentum.
C. A figure of speech in which two unlike things are compared using "like" or "as."
