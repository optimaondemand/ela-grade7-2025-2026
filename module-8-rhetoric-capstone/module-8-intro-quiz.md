Type: essay_question | Points: 0

*Before you begin the module, answer this in your own words — there is no right or wrong answer yet:*

**What makes language powerful enough to move people to action?**

Write 3–5 sentences. You will answer this question again at the end of the module, and your thinking will have changed.
