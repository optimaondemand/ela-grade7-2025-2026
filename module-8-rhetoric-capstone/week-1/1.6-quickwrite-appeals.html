Quickwrite 1.6: What the Dominant Appeal Argues

5 points — Canvas text box submission

Write 5–7 sentences responding to this prompt. Use the required tags below.

Prompt

In 5–7 sentences, explain: in “Give Me Liberty or Give Me Death,” which of the three rhetorical appeals — ethos, pathos, or logos — does the most essential work, and why? Your answer should name at least one specific moment in the speech and explain what that appeal accomplishes that the other two could not accomplish alone. Connect your answer to the essential question: How does a writer use the structure of language to make an argument?

Required Tags

[ETHOS], [PATHOS], or [LOGOS] — before your analysis of the dominant appeal
[APP] — use an appositive to identify Henry’s role or the speech’s function
[EQ] — connect your answer to the essential question: How does a writer use the structure of language to make an argument?

Grading

5 points for a complete response that uses all three tags, names the dominant appeal, and cites at least one specific moment from the speech. 0 points for a missing or off-topic response.
